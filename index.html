VIT's Driving Dataset

VIT’s Driving Dataset
for Indian Roads.

VIT Driving Dataset consisting of 1232 images each for lane segmentation and obstacle detection. Our dataset comprises two sets of images, one for lane segmentation and the other for obstacle detection with 1232 images in each set. The dataset was collected from VIT Vellore Campus, Katpadi, and Vellore city roads all located in Tamil Nadu, India. These locations have a mixture of rural and urban areas, with a combination of single and double-lane roads with a variety of traffic.

Dataset Collection and Annotations Details

The VIT Driving dataset has collection of annotated images that are designed to aid in the development of autonomous driving technologies. The dataset contains images that are taken from a driving perspective and annotated with information about the objects and features present in the scene.

Driving Time

A total of 2 hours were spent with vehicle velocity of 25km-60km.

Distance Covered

Around 60 kilometers of distace travelled during dataset collection

FPS

30 Frames per second (RGB Frame Rate).

Hardware & Resolution
